Driver Controls:
STICK 1
- Button 4: low gear
- Button 5: high gear

Operator Controls:
STICK 3
- Button 2: open gripper
- Button 3: close gripper
- Button 9: go to mush position
STICK 4
- Button 2: extend wheelie bar
- Button 3: retract wheelie bar
- Button 4: lower lift one level
- Button 5: raise lift one level
- Button 8: go to RC pickup position
- Button 9: go to highest position

Stick 3 Trigger + Stick 4 Y-Axis: manually adjust lift
Stick 3 Y-Axis: left roller (- is in, + is out)
Stick 4 Y-Axis: right roller (- is in, + is out)

Piston 0: drive train shift
Piston 1: gripper
Piston 2: wheelie
